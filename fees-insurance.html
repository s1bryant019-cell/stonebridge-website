Payment Options

Insurance and payment options.

Stonebridge accepts BCBS Illinois commercial plans and is actively credentialing with additional insurance networks. If your plan is not active yet, you are still welcome to reach out to discuss timing, private pay, out-of-network benefits, or limited reduced-fee options.

Accepted now

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield of Illinois commercial plans.
  • BCBS PPO, Blue Choice, and Blue High Performance plans.

  • Cigna / Evernorth planned after network reopening.

Private pay and reduced-fee options

If insurance is not active or does not fit, prospective clients may ask about private pay, out-of-network benefits when applicable, and whether limited reduced-fee options are available.

Plans listed as credentialing or enrollment in progress are not yet active for billing. Benefits are verified before insurance billing begins. Some parent/family services may be billable when clinically appropriate and connected to an identified treatment need; educational workshops or groups may be private-pay. Copays, deductibles, coinsurance, non-covered services, and missed or late-canceled appointments may still be the client’s responsibility.

Appointments

Appointment structure.

A brief consultation comes first. Full sessions, billing, and cancellation expectations are clarified before care begins.

Free consultation

The brief 15-minute consultation may occur before the first full clinical session. It is not psychotherapy, a diagnostic evaluation, or crisis care.

First full session

The initial diagnostic evaluation is the first full clinical appointment. Ongoing sessions are billed according to the service provided.

Late cancellations and no-shows

Stonebridge requests at least 24 hours’ notice. Missed or late-canceled appointments may be charged and cannot be billed to insurance.

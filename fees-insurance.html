Appointment Model

How psychotherapy time is structured.

Stonebridge uses a structured appointment model designed to support focused clinical care, accurate documentation, and continuity between sessions.

60-minute appointment blocks

Psychotherapy appointments are scheduled in 60-minute blocks. The direct clinical portion of a standard session typically lasts 45–50 minutes.

The remaining time supports documentation, transition, preparation, and continuity of care.

Built into the care model

This structure protects focused clinical time while allowing space for documentation, transition, preparation, and continuity of care.

  • Clinical time is protected and focused.
  • Documentation and transition time are built into the appointment structure.
  • Sessions may extend when clinically necessary, such as during safety concerns or other significant clinical circumstances.

Insurance

Insurance status.

Stonebridge is working toward participation with select commercial insurance plans. Coverage is confirmed once participation is active and benefits are verified.

Private pay during credentialing

Stonebridge Psychological Group operates as a private-pay telehealth practice while insurance participation is being established with select commercial plans.

Insurance billing begins only after participation is active and formally confirmed.

Future insurance use

Once participation is active with a specific plan, client responsibility may depend on the plan’s copay, deductible, coinsurance, coverage rules, and benefit verification.

Benefit verification helps clarify expected coverage. Clients remain responsible for fees, balances, and non-covered services according to Stonebridge’s financial policies.

Financial Policies

Important payment details.

These policies help protect reserved clinical time and clarify financial expectations before treatment begins.

Out-of-network benefits Some clients may be eligible for partial reimbursement through out-of-network benefits, depending on their plan.
Superbills A superbill may be available upon request when appropriate for private-pay services.
Cancellations and missed appointments Stonebridge requires at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Late cancellations with less than 24 hours’ notice or no-shows may be charged the applicable session fee. Insurance cannot be billed for missed appointments or late cancellations.
Questions about fees, insurance status, reimbursement, or fit can be discussed during consultation. This page provides general information. Signed financial agreements and insurance verification guide final payment responsibilities.

Appointments and payment

Clear expectations protect the frame.

  • The free 10–15 minute consultation may occur before the first full clinical session.
  • The initial diagnostic evaluation is the first full appointment.
  • At least 24 hours’ notice is requested for cancellation or rescheduling.
  • Late cancellations and missed appointments may be charged and cannot be billed to insurance.

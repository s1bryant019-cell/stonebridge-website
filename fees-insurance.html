Payment and insurance

Insurance and payment options.

Accepted now

BCBS Illinois commercial plans

Stonebridge currently accepts BCBS Illinois commercial PPO, Blue Choice, and Blue High Performance plans. This does not include Blue Cross Community Health Plans.

Other ways to pay

Private pay and out-of-network benefits

Private pay, out-of-network reimbursement when applicable, and limited reduced-fee options may be discussed.

What insurance may still leave you responsible for

Copays, deductibles, coinsurance, non-covered services, and missed appointments may still apply. Benefits are verified before billing begins, but verification is not a guarantee of payment.

Appointments and payment

Appointment and cancellation policies.

  • The free 10–15 minute consultation may occur before the first full clinical session.
  • The initial diagnostic evaluation is the first full appointment.
  • At least 24 hours’ notice is requested for cancellation or rescheduling.
  • Late cancellations and missed appointments may be charged and cannot be billed to insurance.

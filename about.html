Meet the founder behind Stonebridge.

Stonebridge Psychological Group is currently led by its founder, with plans for thoughtful growth over time.

Meet the Founder

Stephen W. Bryant

PsyD, LCPC • Founder & Psychologist
Integrative Care Assessment Forensic Perspective Trauma & Mood Concerns

Dr. Bryant provides integrative, client-centered therapy and psychological assessment through a style that is grounded, collaborative, and clinically thoughtful. His work is designed to feel steady and human while maintaining clarity and professional rigor.

Stonebridge was created to bring together depth, structure, and warmth in a practice that can support therapy, evaluation, and future growth. The intention is not simply to offer services, but to create a place that feels substantial, trustworthy, and genuinely helpful.

Areas of Focus

Current strengths within the practice.

Therapy

Assessment

Psychological testing and evaluation to support diagnostic clarity, formulation, and treatment planning.

Practice Growth

A future-facing structure designed to expand into a multi-clinician practice while keeping a clear identity.

About the practice

Built for psychological work that requires steadiness.

Stonebridge Psychological Group is designed around clinical fit, sustainable care, and clear boundaries. The practice is intentionally not a high-volume access machine.

The model

Stonebridge begins with outpatient psychotherapy through telehealth. The website is the public access point; clinical care, records, scheduling, and billing will be organized through the client portal as systems come online.

Good care requires more than availability. It requires attention to fit, scope, boundaries, and the conditions that allow clinicians to remain thoughtful and present.

Client-centered, not indiscriminate

Clients deserve thoughtful care. That includes honest screening when another setting, specialty, or level of support would be more appropriate.

Clinician-centered, not unstructured

Sustainable clinical work depends on documentation, consultation, communication standards, and clear limits around availability.

Growth-oriented, not growth at all costs

The practice may grow, but not by reproducing the overextension that often weakens clinical care.

Current scope

Telehealth psychotherapy is the active service.

Assessment and forensic services are part of the broader professional model, but they are not open for new requests. The current public pathway is psychotherapy inquiry and consultation.

Looking for therapy?

Start with a brief request. Stonebridge will follow up if the inquiry appears appropriate for the practice.

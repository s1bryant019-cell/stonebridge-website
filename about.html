About

A practice built on clarity, depth, and thoughtful care.

Stonebridge Psychological Group brings together clinicians who work from thoughtful, depth-oriented, and evidence-informed approaches to care.

Practice Identity

While approaches may vary across clinicians, the shared emphasis is on clarity, careful thinking, and meaningful psychological work. The practice is intentionally designed to grow into a multi-clinician group while maintaining a strong and coherent identity.

Approach to Care

Work at Stonebridge is grounded in an interest in patterns, relationships, and the deeper meanings that shape emotional experience. At the same time, care remains practical, attuned, and responsive to the present—balancing depth with clarity and direction.

Each clinician brings a distinct approach, while maintaining a shared commitment to thoughtful, grounded, and clinically rigorous care.

Practice Development

Stonebridge is designed to grow intentionally over time. Clinicians whose work aligns with the practice’s emphasis on depth, clarity, and professionalism may be a strong fit as the group expands.

Meet the Founder

Stephen W. Bryant

PsyD, LCPC • Founder & Psychologist
Primary Psychoanalytic OrientationAssessmentForensic PerspectiveTrauma & Mood Concerns

Dr. Bryant provides psychotherapy and psychological assessment through a primary psychoanalytic orientation, supported by a broader integrative clinical framework. His work emphasizes depth, careful listening, emotional meaning, and a thoughtful understanding of how longstanding patterns shape present experience.

Stonebridge was created to bring together depth, structure, and warmth in a practice that can support therapy, evaluation, and future growth. The intention is to build a clinically substantial practice that remains human, grounded, and professionally rigorous.

Professional offerings

01 · Monthly group

A steady space for difficult outpatient work.

Open to mental-health clinicians inside and outside Stonebridge who want thoughtful discussion with a consistent professional group.

  • Third Friday of each month
  • 4:00 PM Central Time
  • Held by secure video
  • Participation reviewed for scope, timing, and group composition
02 · Individual consultation

Focused discussion by inquiry and scope.

Individual consultation may be available for clinical judgment, case conceptualization, boundaries, relational dynamics, risk, or sustainable practice concerns.

Peer review

Professional reflection with a defined purpose.

Peer review may be available when the request, materials, role, and intended use are clear in advance.

Education

Presentations and continuing education.

Educational presentations, webinars, or continuing-education events may be available depending on topic, audience, scope, and scheduling.

Professional scope

Consultation supports judgment. It does not replace required supervision.

This pathway is separate from psychotherapy, employment supervision, and emergency or legal services.

Consultation

Supports reflection, clinical reasoning, professional judgment, and discussion of outpatient work.

Not supervision

Does not substitute for required formal supervision, licensure supervision, or an employer’s supervisory responsibility.

Not emergency or legal support

Does not provide emergency response, legal advice, or direction for matters requiring an attorney, licensing board, employer, or emergency service.

For Clinicians

Consultation and professional learning for thoughtful outpatient work.

Stonebridge offers a separate professional pathway for clinicians interested in relational, ethically grounded consultation and education.

Inquire About Consultation
Monthly consultation group

A steady space for difficult outpatient work.

The group is open to mental health clinicians inside and outside Stonebridge who want thoughtful discussion of clinical judgment, boundaries, relational dynamics, risk, and sustainable practice.

  • Meets on the third Friday of each month
  • 4:00 PM Central Time
  • Held by secure video
  • Participation reviewed for scope, timing, and group composition
Additional professional work

Consultation and education by inquiry.

Individual professional consultation, peer review, and educational presentations may be available depending on scope and scheduling.

This professional pathway is separate from psychotherapy and from employment supervision arrangements.

Email Stonebridge
Professional scope

Consultation supports reflection and professional judgment. It is not emergency support, legal advice, or a substitute for required formal supervision when supervision is needed.

Psychological Assessment

Assessment has a different clinical frame.

Psychological assessment is different from psychotherapy. It involves referral clarification, testing procedures, interpretation, written documentation, and feedback through a separate service pathway.

Assessment pathway

Psychological assessment is part of Stonebridge’s broader professional background and uses a separate process from psychotherapy consultation. Assessment work requires its own referral review, consent, scheduling, documentation, and feedback structure.

Psychotherapy inquiries

Prospective therapy clients may submit a consultation request for outpatient telehealth psychotherapy. Assessment uses a dedicated process so the therapy pathway remains clear and the assessment role remains protected.

Why keep this page?

Assessment Background

Testing should answer a question.

Dr. Bryant’s assessment background includes psychodiagnostic, psychological, neuropsychological, personality, projective, trauma, ADHD, autism, substance use, and differential diagnostic assessment across inpatient, PHP, IOP, and outpatient practice settings.

Future Approach

Parsimonious, hypothesis-driven batteries.

Future assessment work should select the minimum clinician-administered testing needed to answer the referral question, while using self-report and informant measures to gather context without overloading direct testing time.

Possible domains

Cognitive, executive, memory, learning, mood, anxiety, trauma, dissociation, ADHD, autism, personality, and adaptive functioning.

Examples of tools

Depending on age and referral question, future batteries may draw from measures such as WAIS/WISC/WASI, RBANS, D-KEFS, TOVA, CVLT, WMS, MMPI, PAI, Rorschach/R-PAS, TAT, SRS, Vineland, BASC, Conners, BRIEF, PCL-5, or CAPS-5.

Report standard

The goal is clear formulation, defensible findings, and recommendations that people can actually use.

Current Availability

Psychotherapy is the current active service.

If you are seeking psychotherapy, use the consultation form. If you are seeking assessment specifically, Stonebridge is not currently the right referral point.

Clinical & Forensic Psychological Services

A steadier way through complex psychological questions.

Stonebridge Psychological Group provides therapy, psychological assessment, and forensic services for people and situations that require careful attention, clear judgment, and respect for complexity.

The work is warm without becoming vague, clinically serious without becoming cold, and practical without losing sight of the person behind the question.

How the practice is organized

One steady frame, several pathways.

Stonebridge brings together clinical therapy, psychological assessment, and forensic services without blurring their purpose. Each pathway is distinct, but the standard remains the same: careful listening, clear reasoning, and useful psychological work.

Who We Work With

Adults seeking therapy, assessment, consultation, or forensic-informed care in a setting that values depth, clarity, and careful psychological thinking.

What the Practice Offers

Psychotherapy first, with assessment and forensic services clearly defined as complementary service pathways.

Dr. Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · Doctoral-level clinician · Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor, Illinois

Dr. Bryant provides outpatient telehealth psychotherapy for teens and adults located in Illinois. His work is relational, insight-oriented, and steady, with attention to emotional complexity, life history, current stressors, and patterns that can become difficult to change alone.

  • Works withTeens and adults located in Illinois.
  • FormatOutpatient telehealth psychotherapy through a consultation-first process.
  • Clinical focusAnxiety, depression, trauma responses, grief, relationship patterns, identity and self-worth, stress and burnout, substance use concerns when outpatient care fits, and feeling stuck.

Founder · Illinois telehealth

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Psychotherapist and parent consultant

Dr. Bryant provides thoughtful relational psychotherapy for teens and adults, with particular depth in addiction and substance-use concerns, trauma and complex stress, mood and anxiety concerns, and co-occurring patterns. His broader work may also include relationships, identity, grief, emotional regulation, neurodevelopmental concerns, family stress, and parent consultation when clinically appropriate.

  • Addiction and co-occurring concerns
  • Trauma and complex stress
  • Mood, anxiety, and emotional regulation
  • Parent consultation and family stress

Meet the clinicians of Stonebridge Psychological Group.

Stonebridge is intentionally structured to support both current clinical leadership and future growth. As the practice expands, clinicians may be grouped by role while still remaining part of a cohesive, thoughtful practice identity.

Founder

The founder section highlights current clinical leadership and the primary professional identity of Stonebridge Psychological Group.

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder Psychologist

Dr. Bryant is the founder of Stonebridge Psychological Group. His work is grounded in a primarily psychoanalytic orientation, while also encompassing broader clinical and forensic psychological services.

  • Individual therapy and clinically thoughtful care
  • Psychological assessment and diagnostic clarification
  • Forensic and consultation-oriented work
  • Practice leadership and future group development

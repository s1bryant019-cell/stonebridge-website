Meet the clinicians of Stonebridge Psychological Group.

Stonebridge is currently led by its founder, with space intentionally built for additional clinicians whose styles, specialties, and approaches will complement the broader practice.

Future clinicians will bring distinct perspectives and areas of focus while maintaining a shared commitment to thoughtful, grounded, and clinically rigorous care.

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder & Psychologist

Dr. Bryant's work is grounded primarily in a psychoanalytic orientation, with attention to relational patterns, emotional depth, and the meanings that shape a person's inner life. His practice also includes assessment and a forensic perspective, allowing Stonebridge to hold both depth and professional rigor.

  • Primary psychoanalytic orientation
  • Psychotherapy for adults and older adolescents
  • Psychological assessment and diagnostic clarification
  • Trauma, mood concerns, identity, and life transitions

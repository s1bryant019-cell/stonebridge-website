Founder

Stonebridge is currently directed by its founder, whose training and clinical background shape the practice model.

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · PsyD, LCPC

Dr. Bryant founded Stonebridge Psychological Group to support careful, thoughtful, and clinically grounded psychological work. Stonebridge is currently accepting psychotherapy inquiries, while assessment and forensic services remain part of the broader professional model.

  • Telehealth psychotherapy for adults in Illinois
  • Clinical work informed by trauma, addiction, mood, anxiety, neurodevelopmental, and relational concerns
  • Background in psychological assessment, differential diagnosis, and forensic psychology
  • Practice leadership, consultation, and clinician education

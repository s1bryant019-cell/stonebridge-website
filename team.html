Fit and scope

Matching is part of the care model.

Before a first appointment, Stonebridge reviews whether the presenting concern, age, Illinois location, telehealth format, urgency, and clinical scope appear appropriate for outpatient psychotherapy.

If another setting would be more responsible — such as crisis care, in-person treatment, psychological testing, court-related evaluation, or a higher level of care — that will be named as clearly as possible.

Not open bookingProspective clients begin with a waitlist inquiry rather than immediate self-scheduling.
Not one-size-fits-allClinical fit, scope, urgency, and telehealth appropriateness are reviewed before care begins.
Not a crisis pathwayStonebridge is structured for outpatient psychotherapy, not emergency or immediate crisis response.

How matching begins.

Submitting a waitlist inquiry does not establish a clinical relationship or guarantee treatment. It gives Stonebridge enough information to review whether outpatient telehealth psychotherapy may be an appropriate starting point.

Fit

Stonebridge reviews the presenting concern, age, Illinois location, telehealth appropriateness, urgency, and whether outpatient therapy appears clinically appropriate.

Scope

If a different specialty, in-person service, crisis support, or higher level of care is needed, another setting may be recommended.

Next step

If the practice appears clinically appropriate, Stonebridge will follow up regarding availability, insurance status, and possible consultation.

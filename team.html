Founder

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · Clinical Psychologist Postdoctoral / LCPC

Stonebridge is currently led by Dr. Bryant, whose work centers on telehealth psychotherapy for adults in Illinois. The practice is intentionally small, clinically grounded, and structured around careful fit rather than high-volume intake.

His background in trauma, substance use, complex presentations, assessment, and forensic psychology informs the broader direction of the practice, while psychotherapy remains the only active service pathway at this time.

Dr. Bryant provides relational psychotherapy for Illinois clients navigating addiction and substance use concerns, trauma responses, mood and anxiety concerns, and co-occurring patterns that can become difficult to untangle alone. He also offers parent consultation and family-based support when clinically appropriate.

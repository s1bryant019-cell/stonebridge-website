Founder

Dr. Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · Insight-oriented telehealth psychotherapy for teens and adults in Illinois

Dr. Bryant works with clients seeking therapy that is thoughtful, grounded, and attentive to emotional complexity, stress, relationships, trauma history, substance use concerns, identity, and life transitions.

His clinical background includes doctoral training in clinical psychology, graduate training in clinical and forensic psychology, psychological assessment experience, and work across outpatient, intensive outpatient, partial hospitalization, and inpatient behavioral health settings.

A deliberately selected clinical model

Stonebridge is built around careful fit rather than immediate availability. The team model is intended to remain selective, clinically coherent, and aligned with sustainable care.

  • Clear scope before scheduling
  • Clinician fit over immediate availability
  • Boundaries that support reliable care

Begin with consultation

Prospective psychotherapy clients may submit a consultation request. The consultation process helps determine whether Stonebridge is an appropriate outpatient telehealth setting before care begins.

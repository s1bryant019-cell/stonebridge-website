Founder

The founder section highlights current clinical leadership and the professional background shaping the Stonebridge model.

Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · PsyD, LCPC

Dr. Bryant founded Stonebridge Psychological Group to support careful, bounded, clinically serious psychological work. Stonebridge is currently accepting psychotherapy inquiries, with assessment and forensic services remaining part of the broader professional model for future development.

  • Telehealth psychotherapy for adults in Illinois
  • Clinical work informed by trauma, addiction, mood, anxiety, neurodevelopmental, and relational concerns
  • Background in psychological assessment, differential diagnosis, and forensic psychology
  • Practice leadership, consultation, and clinician education

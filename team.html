Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder · Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor, Illinois

Dr. Bryant provides careful relational psychotherapy for teens and adults located in Illinois. His work is steady, thoughtful, and attentive to emotional complexity, stress, relationships, trauma responses, grief, identity, self-worth, substance use concerns when outpatient care fits, and patterns that feel hard to change.

  • Works with teens and adults located in Illinois.
  • Clinical focus includes anxiety, depression, trauma responses, grief, relationships, identity, self-worth, stress, substance use concerns when outpatient care fits, and feeling stuck.
  • Care is provided through outpatient telehealth psychotherapy.

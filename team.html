How team fit works.

Stonebridge does not use open booking. A brief inquiry or consultation helps determine whether the practice, clinician, telehealth format, and level of care appear appropriate before therapy begins.

What is reviewed

Age, location in Illinois, presenting concerns, telehealth fit, urgency, insurance or payment questions, and whether outpatient psychotherapy appears appropriate.

What is not promised

A waitlist inquiry does not establish a clinical relationship, guarantee an appointment, or replace emergency or crisis support.

When another setting fits better

If a different specialty, in-person care, urgent support, or a higher level of care is needed, referral guidance may be offered when possible.

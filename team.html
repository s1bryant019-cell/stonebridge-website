Stephen W. Bryant, PsyD, LCPC

Founder & Psychologist

Dr. Bryant's work is grounded primarily in a psychoanalytic orientation, with attention to relational patterns, emotional depth, and the meanings that shape a person's inner life. His practice also includes assessment and a forensic perspective, allowing Stonebridge to hold both depth and professional rigor.

Clear pathways, carefully held.

Stonebridge is currently accepting psychotherapy inquiries. Assessment and forensic services remain part of the broader practice model, but they are not currently available for new requests. The goal is to be clear about what is available now while preserving the structure for future growth.

Current availability

Psychotherapy is active.

Consultation requests are currently open for psychotherapy only. Assessment and forensic pages are included for orientation, but those services are not accepting new requests at this time.

Currently available

Psychotherapy

Individual psychotherapy is the active clinical service at Stonebridge. The work is intended for adults seeking a thoughtful, bounded, and clinically serious setting for sustained psychological care.

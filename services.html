Services offered

Two primary ways Stonebridge supports clients and families.

Individual care

Individual Psychotherapy

Steady, relational work for people who want to understand what is happening beneath symptoms and practice more workable ways of responding.

  • Teens and adults located in Illinois
  • Outpatient telehealth format
  • Insight, emotional processing, and practical tools when useful
Caregiver and family support

Parent Consultation & Parenting Support

Support for caregivers trying to understand patterns, strengthen connection, and respond with more clarity and steadiness.

  • Individual parent consultation
  • Family-based or parent-child sessions when appropriate
  • Small consultation groups when available
Concerns often addressed

Problems rarely arrive one at a time.

Stonebridge looks for the pattern beneath the list rather than forcing experience into a single isolated category.

Emotional distress

Anxiety, depression, panic, irritability, numbness, shutdown, avoidance, or feeling overwhelmed.

Trauma and shame

Threat responses, trust, body-based distress, grief, self-worth, and protective patterns shaped by earlier experiences.

Relationships and identity

Conflict, disconnection, people-pleasing, attachment patterns, identity questions, and difficulty naming needs.

Coping and stuckness

Burnout, stress, substance-use concerns when outpatient care fits, and cycles that have not changed through effort alone.

How Stonebridge works

Thoughtful care with enough structure to be useful.

The relationship matters

Therapy is not reduced to a checklist. The therapeutic relationship is part of the work.

Pace and readiness matter

Safety, consent, timing, and the client’s capacity shape how the work proceeds.

Insight can lead to action

Skills, structure, and planning may be integrated when they support meaningful change.

Fit and scope

Stonebridge provides outpatient telehealth care. Emergency needs, psychological testing, legal or court-related evaluations, and concerns requiring a higher level of care use different pathways.

Begin with a brief fit review.

Share what you are looking for so scope, telehealth appropriateness, and next steps can be considered carefully.

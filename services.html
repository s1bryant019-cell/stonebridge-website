A clearer path into care

The services page is meant to simplify the first step. Rather than overwhelming visitors with too many options at once, Stonebridge organizes its work into a few clearly defined pathways so people can understand where to begin.

If you are unsure which service best fits your needs, a consultation request can help clarify whether psychotherapy, assessment, or another referral direction may be most appropriate.

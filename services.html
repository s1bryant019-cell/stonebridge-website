Current pathway

Psychotherapy

Telehealth psychotherapy is the current clinical service at Stonebridge. The work is insight-oriented, fit-conscious, and structured around steady, thoughtful care.

The consultation process helps determine whether outpatient telehealth is clinically appropriate and whether Stonebridge is the right setting before care begins.

Professional scope

Assessment and forensic services

Psychological assessment and forensic services are distinct areas of professional practice. They require separate intake procedures, informed consent, documentation standards, timelines, role clarification, and conflict review.

These services are separate from the psychotherapy consultation form. When offered, they use separate processes rather than the standard therapy pathway.

How to begin

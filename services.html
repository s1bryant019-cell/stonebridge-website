Visible, but not open for new requests.

Assessment and forensic services are included so visitors can understand the wider Stonebridge model. They are not currently available for scheduling, and the consultation form blocks those request types for now.

This protects client time, keeps expectations clear, and avoids implying availability before the necessary clinical and administrative systems are in place.

Start with an initial psychotherapy inquiry.

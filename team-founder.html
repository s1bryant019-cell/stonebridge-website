Clinician Profile

Dr. Stephen W. Bryant

PsyD, LCPC · Founder · Counselor · Telehealth psychotherapy for adults in Illinois

Dr. Bryant works with adults navigating anxiety, trauma history, emotional disconnection, substance use concerns, grief, identity questions, relationship patterns, and major life transitions.

His approach is steady, collaborative, and reflective. The first step is a brief consultation to determine whether outpatient telehealth psychotherapy at Stonebridge is the right fit.

Current limits

People often reach out when life looks manageable from the outside, but feels harder internally.

Therapy may focus on stress, anxiety, emotional fatigue, trauma history, identity development, grief, substance use concerns, relationship patterns, or transitions that are affecting day-to-day life.

Specialties and concerns

Anxiety Trauma and PTSD Mood concerns Substance use Stress and burnout Grief Relationship issues Life transitions Identity and self-esteem

Treatment approach

Dr. Bryant integrates psychodynamic and relational therapy with practical skills from CBT, DBT, ACT, motivational interviewing, and mindfulness-based approaches.

Good fit

A good fit may include adults seeking outpatient telehealth psychotherapy who can attend privately, are not in immediate crisis, and want a therapy process that is thoughtful, steady, and clinically grounded.

Not the right fit

Stonebridge is not appropriate for immediate danger, active emergency needs, mandated forensic work, current psychological assessment requests, or situations requiring in-person or higher-level care.

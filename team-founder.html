Clinician Profile

Dr. Stephen W. Bryant

PsyD, LCPC · Founder · Telehealth psychotherapy for adults in Illinois

Dr. Bryant works with adults who are carrying stress, anxiety, burnout, relationship patterns, grief, trauma history, substance use concerns, or major life transitions that have become difficult to sort through alone.

Sessions are intended to be thoughtful, steady, and collaborative. The work begins with understanding what is happening, what has helped before, what has not, and whether outpatient telehealth is the right level of care.

Accepting psychotherapy inquiries

At a glance

  • Adults only
  • Telehealth only
  • Client must be located in Illinois
  • Psychotherapy is the active service
  • Consultation before scheduling

A consultation request is not therapy and does not establish a clinical relationship. It is the first step in determining fit.

People often reach out when life looks manageable from the outside, but feels harder internally.

Therapy may focus on stress, anxiety, emotional fatigue, relationship patterns, identity questions, grief, trauma history, substance use concerns, or transitions that are affecting day-to-day life.

Therapy style

Dr. Bryant’s style is collaborative and psychologically curious. Sessions may include reflection, pattern recognition, emotional processing, practical coping, and attention to experiences that feel hard to name clearly.

Good fit

A good fit may include adults seeking outpatient telehealth psychotherapy who can attend privately, are not in immediate crisis, and want a therapy process that is steady, reflective, and clinically grounded.

Not the right fit

Stonebridge is not appropriate for immediate danger, active emergency needs, mandated forensic work, current psychological assessment requests, or situations requiring in-person or higher-level care.

Next step

Submit a brief consultation request. The request will be reviewed before therapy is scheduled.

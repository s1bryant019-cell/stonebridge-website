About

Stephen founded Stonebridge to create a practice where clinical quality, ethical boundaries, and clinician sustainability are treated as part of the same system. The work is patient-facing, restrained, and focused on appropriate fit.

Approach to therapy

The therapy stance is thoughtful and collaborative. Sessions are intended to support reflection, emotional clarity, and practical movement without turning care into generic advice or a rushed checklist.

Professional background

Stephen’s background includes psychotherapy, psychological practice development, and attention to the systems that shape ethical clinical work. Full credential and insurance details can be refined as systems come online.

Fit / not a fit

Stonebridge may be a fit for adults seeking outpatient telehealth psychotherapy in Illinois. It is not a fit for emergencies, immediate crisis care, court-ordered forensic needs, open psychological testing requests, or services requiring in-person care.

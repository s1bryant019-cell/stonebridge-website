About

Stephen founded Stonebridge to create a practice where clinical care, boundaries, and sustainability are built into the structure rather than treated as afterthoughts.

Approach to therapy

The work is collaborative and psychologically curious. Sessions may explore symptoms, patterns, stressors, relationships, identity, trauma history, coping, and the parts of life that have become difficult to hold alone.

Professional background

Stephen’s background informs a practice model that treats fit, ethical boundaries, and clinician sustainability as part of clinical quality.

Good fit / Not a fit

Stonebridge may be a good fit for adults seeking outpatient telehealth psychotherapy in Illinois. It is not appropriate for immediate crisis needs, mandated forensic work, current assessment requests, or needs requiring in-person or higher-level care.

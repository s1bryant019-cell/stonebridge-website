A

About

Dr. Bryant is the founder of Stonebridge Psychological Group. His clinical background includes work across inpatient, partial hospitalization, intensive outpatient, outpatient, and private-practice settings with adolescents and adults.

His current practice focuses on adult psychotherapy through telehealth, with attention to history, context, symptoms, relationships, and the ways people adapt to difficulty over time.

T

Approach

Therapy is not built around quick reassurance or generic advice. The work emphasizes depth, clarity, careful pacing, and a treatment relationship that is warm, direct, and appropriately bounded.

Sessions may focus on trauma, substance use, mood and anxiety concerns, identity, relational patterns, and questions that require more nuanced psychological understanding.

B

Background

  • Experience across inpatient, PHP, IOP, outpatient, and private-practice settings
  • Assessment-informed clinical thinking and diagnostic formulation
  • Graduate training in forensic psychology and court-involved system experience

Assessment and forensic experience inform the clinical thinking behind the practice. Stonebridge is not currently accepting new assessment or forensic requests.

F

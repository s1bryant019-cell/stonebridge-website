Active clients may access TherapyPortal for assigned paperwork, routine forms, appointment-related details, billing-related items when available, and scheduled telehealth sessions.

Access Client Portal

Assigned forms

After consultation, active clients may be asked to complete intake paperwork, consent forms, insurance or payment forms, and clinically appropriate questionnaires through TherapyPortal.

Materials are assigned based on clinical relevance and stage of care.

Telehealth

Telehealth access is intended for scheduled appointments. Portal use supports routine client care and administrative coordination.

Routine use only

Portal access is intended for active clients and routine administrative use. Emergency and crisis needs require immediate support outside the portal.

For immediate danger or a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental health, suicide/self-harm, or substance-use crisis support, call or text 988.

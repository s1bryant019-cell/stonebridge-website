Active client use

Routine care tasks, kept secure.

The portal is for assigned client tasks after Stonebridge has connected with you. It keeps private materials out of the public website and in the secure client system.

Paperwork Complete forms or questionnaires assigned by the practice.
Sessions Access scheduled telehealth sessions when portal access is active.
Account items Review billing-related items when those features are available for your account.
Privacy Use the portal for assigned routine tasks rather than sending sensitive details through the website.

Routine use

Use the portal for tasks connected to active care.

TherapyPortal is the secure location for assigned forms, scheduled telehealth sessions, and routine account items. Use it instead of the public website for sensitive details connected to ongoing care.

Access Client Portal

Privacy and access

  • Use your own secure login.
  • Complete only items assigned to your account.
  • Keep telehealth links and documents private.
  • Contact Stonebridge if you cannot access an assigned item.
Not for emergencies

The portal is not monitored as an emergency service. For immediate danger or a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental-health, suicide, self-harm, or substance-use crisis support, call or text 988.

New to Stonebridge?

Begin with a waitlist inquiry rather than creating or using an active-client portal account.

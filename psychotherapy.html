Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy offered with steadiness, depth, and care.

Psychotherapy is the current active service at Stonebridge Psychological Group. The work is meant to feel grounded, thoughtful, and clinically serious, while still remaining humane and approachable.

How therapy is approached

The practice is informed by a primarily psychoanalytic orientation, while also remaining flexible enough to meet the realities of contemporary clinical work. Therapy is understood as a space for reflection, understanding, change, and more thoughtful ways of relating to oneself and others.

Thoughtful, insight-oriented treatment
Attention to emotional patterns and meaning
A steady and professional therapeutic frame

Who psychotherapy may serve

Psychotherapy may be appropriate for adults seeking support around mood, anxiety, relational difficulties, life transitions, identity, grief, self-understanding, or more longstanding emotional concerns.

Current format

Psychotherapy is currently offered via telehealth. This allows the practice to remain accessible while preserving a consistent and contained therapeutic setting.

Beginning the process

The first step is a consultation request. That initial inquiry helps determine whether therapy at Stonebridge appears clinically appropriate and whether a consultation should be scheduled.

Starting therapy at Stonebridge

Psychotherapy

Therapy with depth, steadiness, and fit.

Stonebridge currently offers outpatient psychotherapy through telehealth for clients located in Illinois.

Current therapy pathway

Prospective clients begin with a consultation request. If the inquiry appears appropriate, Stonebridge may schedule a brief consultation to clarify fit, goals, telehealth appropriateness, availability, and next steps.

The consultation is not psychotherapy and does not establish a clinical relationship.

Best aligned for

  • Adults seeking outpatient psychotherapy
  • Clients located in Illinois during sessions
  • Concerns appropriate for telehealth care
  • People looking for thoughtful, consistent work

Clinical fit

Stonebridge does not use open booking. A fit-focused process helps avoid placing clients in care that may not match their needs.

Telehealth boundaries

Sessions require privacy, an appropriate location, and presence in a state where the clinician can legally provide services.

Routine care

Stonebridge is not an emergency or crisis service. Email, forms, portal messages, and voicemail are not for urgent safety needs.

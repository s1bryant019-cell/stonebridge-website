Waitlist inquiries

Join the psychotherapy waitlist.

Stonebridge is accepting waitlist inquiries for outpatient telehealth psychotherapy. The practice is currently awaiting confirmation of insurance paneling with select commercial plans and anticipates beginning online psychotherapy appointments around August 2026.

A brief note is enough. If the practice appears clinically appropriate, Stonebridge will follow up regarding availability, insurance status, and next steps.

Join the WaitlistView fees & insurance

Submitting this form does not establish a clinical relationship or guarantee availability.

The form gives Stonebridge the information needed to review fit, scope, telehealth appropriateness, and payment or insurance preferences.

Before you submit

  • This form is for outpatient telehealth psychotherapy waitlist inquiries and consultation next steps.
  • Services are telehealth only for teens and adults located in Illinois.
  • A clinical relationship begins only after consultation, consent, and intake are completed.

Before submitting a request, you may review fees and insurance information.

If Stonebridge appears clinically appropriate and availability becomes clearer, intake paperwork, consent forms, and clinically relevant questionnaires may be coordinated through TherapyPortal.

What to include

A short description is enough. You do not need to share highly detailed or sensitive history in this form.

Emergency and crisis needs: This form is not monitored for emergencies. Immediate danger or medical emergencies require 911 or the nearest emergency room. Mental health crisis support is available by calling or texting 988.

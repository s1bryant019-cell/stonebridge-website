Before you submit

  • Psychotherapy is the active consultation pathway at Stonebridge.
  • Services are telehealth only for teens and adults located in Illinois.
  • Submitting a request does not establish a clinical relationship.

Before submitting a request, you may review current fees and insurance information.

If Stonebridge appears appropriate after consultation, intake paperwork, consent forms, and clinically relevant questionnaires may be coordinated through TherapyPortal.

What to include

A short description is enough. Please do not include detailed trauma history, highly sensitive information, or urgent safety concerns in this form.

Emergency boundary: This form is not monitored for emergencies. Immediate danger or medical emergencies require 911 or the nearest emergency room. Mental health crisis support is available by calling or texting 988.

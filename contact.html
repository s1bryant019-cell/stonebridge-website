Keep it simple.

The waitlist form is only a starting point. You do not need to share your full history here.

  • What to includeA brief reason for seeking therapy, the proposed client’s name, and your preferred way to be contacted.
  • What it doesThe form helps Stonebridge review clinical fit, scope, telehealth appropriateness, and payment or insurance preferences.
  • What it does not doSubmitting the form does not establish a clinical relationship or guarantee availability.
Emergency and crisis needs: This form is not monitored for emergencies. For immediate danger or medical emergencies, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. Mental health crisis support is available by calling or texting 988.

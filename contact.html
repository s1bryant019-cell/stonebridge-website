Use this form for non-emergency psychotherapy consultation requests. Stonebridge currently provides telehealth psychotherapy for teens and adults located in Illinois.

Before you submit

  • Psychotherapy is currently the active service at Stonebridge.
  • Services are telehealth only for teens and adults located in Illinois.
  • This form is not monitored for emergencies or crisis support.
  • Submitting a request does not establish a clinical relationship.

What to include

A short description is enough. Please do not include detailed trauma history, highly sensitive information, or urgent safety concerns in this form.

Emergency boundary: If there is immediate danger or a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For mental health, suicide/self-harm, or substance-use crisis support in Illinois or Chicago, call or text 988.

Request a consultation.

This page is intended to make initial outreach clearer and more useful. Stonebridge offers a free 15-minute consultation to help determine whether the practice may be a good fit and, when appropriate, to help guide referrals if another provider or setting appears better suited.

For now, the form below is a design-forward intake structure. Later, it can be connected so inquiry submissions are sent directly to your email and used as part of a more thoughtful screening and fit process.

The goal of this first step is to gather enough information to respond thoughtfully while keeping the process approachable and not overwhelming.
